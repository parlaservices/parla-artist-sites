Prana Tattoo is home to six resident artists working in Bondi Beach, Sydney. Each artist runs their own practice and bookings. You’ll find their full portfolios, bios, and Instagram handles on their individual pages.

Between them, the roster covers illustrative black and grey, realism and custom script, ornamental, geometry, fine line and blackwork, fine line and sacred geometry, intricate small-scale composition, neo ornamental, black and grey, fine line and geometric work, and freehand illustrative work.

The studio is at 101 O’Brien St, Bondi Beach, NSW 2026. Open 10am to 6pm every day by appointment.
