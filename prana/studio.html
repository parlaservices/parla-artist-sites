Prana represents the exchange of energy into form; art translated onto skin, carried between bodies.

We are a tattoo studio based in Bondi Beach, Sydney, specialising in fine line, blackwork, ornamental, and custom tattooing. You come to us with an idea, and we work with you to develop it into a tattoo that holds.

Your piece is built in context, with placement, scale, and detail resolved on the body so the work feels considered and specific to you.

We work within the traditions of tattooing, with respect for its cultural origins and its evolution, carrying the practice forward with care and precision.

Thank you for entrusting us with this experience, we can’t wait to share this with you.

Getting to the studio.

By car

On-street parking is available on O’Brien Street and the surrounding blocks. Check signage for restrictions. Most spots in the area are metered or time-limited during the day.

By bus

Plenty of bus routes run through Bondi Beach. The 333, 380, and 381 all stop within a short walk of the studio, with frequent services from Bondi Junction, the city, and the inner east.

By train

The nearest station is Bondi Junction, about a ten-minute walk or a short bus ride from the studio. Trains run direct from the city via the T4 line.

On foot

If you’re already in Bondi, we’re a five-minute walk from the beach. Up O’Brien Street from Campbell Parade, on the corner.
