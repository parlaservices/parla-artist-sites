A custom tattoo studio in the heart of Bondi Beach.

Prana Tattoo is a private, fine-art tattoo studio in the heart of Bondi Beach, Sydney. Six resident artists work across varied tattoo styles such as fine line, ornamental, realism, micro-realism, illustrative and black & grey.

How we work

Each of our six artists specialises in custom designs, and we work on everything from smaller pieces through to large multi-session pieces.

The best way to book is to reach out to each artist directly: have a look through their profile below and get in touch via their profile page or their Instagram. Each artist runs their own bookings and will respond personally.

The artists

Charlie Lund. Specialising in illustrative black and grey, realism and custom script. Charlie is also a renowned visual artist with her work being featured at the State Library of New South Wales.

Josh Marx. Ornamental, geometry, fine line and blackwork. Also a renowned artist, Josh has a parallel painting practice that informs his unique compositions.

Caroline Lindskov. Fine line and sacred geometry, rooted in astrology. Caroline draws inspiration from her spiritual practice and yoga teaching to create deeply meaningful works with her clients.

Konrad Tomimura. Fine line and blackwork. Konrad's unique designs traverse a range of different subject matter, and are always highly sought after.

Chris Moit. Neo ornamental, black and grey, fine line and geometric. Chris is known for his complex and detailed pieces that flow effortlessly with the body.

Izzy Antonievich. Fine line, intricate and illustrative. Izzy draws inspiration from the dream world and her practice as a musician to create a distinctive style that has garnered a loyal client base.

Inside the studio

The studio is a beautiful shared space designed around one thing: making each client comfortable. Well-lit work stations, a relaxed but focused atmosphere, and the time and care to do every piece properly.

Our standards on hygiene and safety are uncompromising. Single-use needles, sterilised equipment, and the full set of certifications you'd expect from a Sydney studio.
