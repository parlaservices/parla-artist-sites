Travel from Malang to Mount Bromo, Banyuwangi, Ijen, Pupuan, and Ubud on a journey that moves beyond Indonesia's landmarks and into the stories that surround them. Alongside volcano sunrises and celebrated destinations are village roads, ferry crossings, farming communities, creative workshops, and encounters that illuminate Indonesia's past and present. From traces of colonial history to traditions that continue to shape daily life, the route is designed to offer a deeper understanding of the places it passes through.
